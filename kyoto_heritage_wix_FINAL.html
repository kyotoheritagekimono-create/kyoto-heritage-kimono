OUR STORY

"Not just a garment.
A century of craft, still breathing."

Japan's vintage kimono and haori have been disappearing — stored in tansu chests, passed between antique dealers, or simply lost. We founded Kyoto Heritage Kimono in 2025 to change that. Based in Kyoto, we personally seek out pieces of remarkable craftsmanship and beauty.

We are at the beginning of our journey. Every piece we offer has been thoughtfully selected with genuine love for Japanese textile culture — one extraordinary piece at a time.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions We Are Happy to Answer

Where can I purchase your pieces?

+

Our folding fans are available on our Etsy shop at kyotoheritagekimono.etsy.com. For haori, please contact us by email. We are building direct checkout on this site — sign up to our newsletter to be notified when it launches.

How do you ship your pieces?

+

We pack every piece with genuine care — wrapped carefully to protect it during transit. We ship internationally and take the packaging process seriously, because we know each piece is special. For any questions about shipping to your location, please contact us before purchasing.

Do the pieces come with any documentation?

+

At this stage, we do not issue formal certificates of authenticity. What we provide is a genuine, detailed description of each piece — its technique, materials, and what makes it special — along with accurate photographs. We are transparent about everything we know.

How do you select the pieces in your collection?

+

Every piece is personally hand-selected in Kyoto, Japan. We look for genuine craftsmanship, beautiful motifs, and pieces that carry a sense of history. We do not list anything we would not be proud to give as a gift.

What is the price for the haori robes?

+

Haori pricing is available on request, as each piece is unique. Please contact us at kyoto.heritage.kimono@gmail.com and we will be happy to share pricing and additional photographs.

Can I return a purchase?

+

For Etsy purchases, please refer to our Etsy shop policies. For direct enquiries, we handle each situation individually and always aim to find a fair solution. If a piece is significantly different from how it was described, please contact us and we will do our best to make it right.

How should I care for a silk haori or folding fan?

+

For silk haori, we recommend dry cleaning only and storing away from direct sunlight and moisture. For folding fans, keep them away from humidity and store flat or in their original sleeve if available. We are happy to share care tips specific to each piece.
